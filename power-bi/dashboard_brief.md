# Dashboard Brief - Retail Demand Forecasting

## Audience and purpose

This dashboard is for a retail operations or inventory planning stakeholder. It should connect forecast accuracy to safety stock and reorder-point decisions.

## Pages

### 1 - Executive KPI

Show best model, WMAPE, operating-cost comparison, total demand trend, and model metrics.

### 2 - Diagnostic Analysis

Show demand volatility by store and item, hardest SKU/store combinations, and forecast error by dimension.

### 3 - Decision Support

Rank reorder-point value exposures, safety stock units, and SKU difficulty so a planner can prioritize review.

## Honesty notes

The Kaggle dataset has unit demand only. Inventory costs, unit margin, holding cost, reorder cost, and service-level assumptions are planning assumptions documented in the Python config and outputs.
